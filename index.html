Why try this?

It's not forever

Each challenge will run for just 30 days. Long enough to make a difference. Not so long that you'll want to give up.

You'll be suffering with others

Others will be doing the same - suffering horribly, celebrating success. Knowing you're not alone makes a huge difference.

Celebrate what you DON'T do

Sometimes the hardest thing is saying no. We'll acknowledge that you turned down the free doughnuts in the kitchen!

It's fun!

Building on little successes and getting constant feedback and encouragement will make you feel amazing!
